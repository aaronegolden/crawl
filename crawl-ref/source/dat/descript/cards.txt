%%%%
# Escape
Velocity card

This card makes the user swift and may haste them at high power.
%%%%
the Tomb card

This card entombs its user in temporary rock walls.
%%%%
Exile card

This card attempts to banish hostile monsters, checking magic resistance.
%%%%
the Shaft card

The card depicts a traveler plummeting into a chasm that has opened beneath
them.

Drawing this card will create a shaft beneath the user, and possibly beneath
their enemies.
%%%%
Famine card

This card inflicts a terrible hunger, paralyzing or weakening enemies.
%%%%
the Elixir card

This card restores the user's health and magic for a duration dependent on power.
%%%%
The Squid card

This card wreaths the user in thick, sight-obscuring smoke for a duration.
%%%%
the Cloud card

The card depicts an iron gate enshrouded in mist.

This card imprisons the user's foes in rings of clouds. The type of clouds
produced will improve with card power.
%%%%
# Destruction
Vitriol card

This card corrodes hostile monsters and unleashes a corrosive bolt.
%%%%
the Storm card

This card unleashes a blast of wind and several electrical explosions.
%%%%
Pain card

The card depicts an iron wheel, menacing with spikes and rusted with blood.

Drawing this card unleashes a necromantic attack spell.
%%%%
the Leech card

This card drains life from an enemy, restoring health to the user.
%%%%
the Orb card

This card unleashes multiple orbs of destructive magic.
%%%%
Degeneration card

This card tries to polymorph hostile monsters, halving their health if it succeeds.
%%%%
Wild Magic card

This card causes miscasts on foes and restores the user's magic for each foe hit.
# Summoning
%%%%
the Elements card

This card summons between one and five elementals of the same type.
%%%%
the Pentagram card

This card summons a demon or, with sufficient power, pandemonium lord.
%%%%
the Dance card

The card depicts an extravagantly-dressed, masked figure dancing alone in a
vast room.

Drawing this card will summon a dancing weapon. Its quality, brand, and
friendliness are all affected by card power.
%%%%
Foxfire card

This card summons a horde of killer bees.
%%%%
the Rangers card

This card summons two archers. The quality of the summons increases with power.
%%%%
the Illusion card

This card summons an illusion of the user. Its duration improves with power.
%%%%
The Garden card

This card summons numerous briar patches and, occasionally, oklob plants. 
%%%%
# Punishment
Wrath card

The card depicts the hand of the divine, reaching down from the heavens to hurl
furious lightning.

Drawing this card will draw a god's wrath against the user.
%%%%
the Wraith card

The card depicts absolutely nothing.

Drawing this card will drain the user.
%%%%
the Swine card

The card depicts several feral hogs wallowing in a mud pit.

Drawing this card will make its user turn pink and root for truffles.
%%%%
Torment card

The card depicts a screaming figure being tortured on the rack.

Drawing this card will cause agonising injury to the user.
%%%%
# cards for wizmode testing (&c)
the Stairs card

The card depicts water flowing down a set of stairs.

Drawing this card makes staircases slide towards or away from the user. It is a
bug if one ever finds it in a deck.
%%%%
# buggy cards
a buggy card

Drawing this card is a bug, if one ever encounters it as such.
%%%%
a very buggy card

The card depicts several Crawl players angrily filing several bug reports on
Mantis.

Drawing this card is a bug, if one ever encounters it as such.
